Frequently Asked Questions

Clear, direct answers to help you organize your upcoming celebration with ease

What exactly does complete event management include?

Complete event management at ManiPlanners covers everything required to run a smooth celebration. This includes custom thematic decoration design, hiring professional catering teams, establishing specialized audio-visual tech rigs, mapping creative photography schedules, and deploying expert logistics coordinators on-site to resolve issues in real-time.

Why should I hire an event planner instead of sourcing directly?

Hiring us saves you time, stress, and extra costs. Instead of manually coordinating with separate florists, catering companies, photography crews, and sound technicians, you gain a single point of accountability. We protect your budget using established industry network pricing and ensure all elements align perfectly without timeline clashes.

How does your event pricing system function?

We work flexibly across transparent price points. We offer fixed package solutions for traditional structures like baby showers or housewarmings, alongside fully customized proposals for large-scale premium gatherings. We specialize in striking the right balance—crafting an upscale, high-end feel while protecting your core parameters carefully.

How early should we lock in our event date with ManiPlanners?

To ensure total availability for prime event venues across Bengaluru, we recommend booking our team 4 to 8 weeks in advance. However, if you are coordinating a shorter, urgent schedule, our structural team is fully equipped to deploy premium arrangements on short notice.
